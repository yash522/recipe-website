Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 1 hour

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Add eggs and butter
  3. Mix properly
  4. Pour into baking tray
  5. Bake in oven for 30-40 minutes
