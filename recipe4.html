Vegetable Biryani

Preparation Time: 1 hour

Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Spices
  • Onion
  • Curd

Steps

  1. Cook rice separately
  2. Cook vegetables with spices
  3. Layer rice and vegetables
  4. Cook on low heat (dum)
  5. Serve hot
