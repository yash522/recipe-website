Paneer Butter Masala

Preparation Time: 40 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Paneer cubes
  • Tomato puree
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan
  2. Add tomato puree and cook
  3. Add spices and mix well
  4. Add paneer cubes
  5. Add cream and cook for few minutes
