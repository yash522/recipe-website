Fresh Vegetable Salad

Preparation Time: 15 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables
  2. Mix in a bowl
  3. Add salt and lemon juice
  4. Mix well
  5. Serve fresh
